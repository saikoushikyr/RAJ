Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery timings?

We deliver from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, seven days a week. Orders placed before 9:30 PM will be delivered the same day.

What is the minimum order amount?

There is no minimum order amount. You can order any quantity of products from our store.

Do you accept returns?

Yes, we accept returns within 24 hours of delivery for damaged or incorrect items.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery, UPI, and all major credit/debit cards.
